My dataset includes all current AU athletes their name, sport, high school, hometown, whether they played on a club team and the name of the club team all from their AU profile on the AU athletics website. It also includes whether the high school they attended was public or private. If it was public, the percent of students on free and reduced lunch is included. If it was private, the annual school tuition was included. Links to tuition and free and reduced lunch percentage are included in the dataset for reference. 
I am in the process of adding median household income of the hometown for all of the students who attended public school. The data will be from census data, but the census database only includes towns of a popualtion of at least 5,000. Additional sources for towns that are not in the census database will be linked in another column. 
The spreadsheet also includes some pivot tables. Some of the results include that 1 in every 3 AU athletes attended private school.

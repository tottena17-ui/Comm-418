This is an article from AP News about new data from the Census Bureau about Asian surnames being the fastest growing in the last ten years. I think the article did a great job of analyzing the data and presenting it in a very reader friendly way. He discussed the different names that are in the top ten most popular names as well as breaking the names apart by ethnic/racial backgrounds. His analysis is comprehensive. He also includes quotes from experts in sociology and other fields to make the reporting more well rounded. I think this is a great example of a data story without a visualization.
